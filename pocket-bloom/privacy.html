Pocket Bloom

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-01

Summary

Pocket Bloom is designed as a local, single-player mobile game. The first release does not require an account, does not include advertising, does not include in-app purchases, and does not use cloud sync.

Data Collection

The first release does not intentionally collect personal data from users. Gameplay score, combo, bloom progress, and round status are processed locally during play.

Tracking

Pocket Bloom does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Children and Age Rating

Pocket Bloom is a casual arcade game. If future versions add accounts, social features, leaderboards, ads, purchases, analytics, or cloud services, this policy and the App Store privacy labels must be updated before release.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support page: Pocket Bloom Support.
