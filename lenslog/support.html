Accessibility information

Current Release

LensLog 1.0 is a first iOS release candidate focused on camera capture, on-device receipt text recognition where available, calorie estimates, expense estimates, user confirmation, local records, export, single-record deletion, and clearing local records.

Important Limits

  • Calorie values are estimates and should be checked by the user; they are not medical or nutrition treatment advice.
  • Expense values are estimates and should be checked against the actual receipt; they are not accounting, tax, banking, investment, or financial advice.
  • Text recognition quality depends on photo clarity, lighting, receipt layout, and device support.
  • The first release is a personal lifestyle logging utility, not a professional health, accounting, tax, banking, investment, or financial product.
