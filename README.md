# BEXS Mobile App Support

Public privacy policy and support pages for App Store review.

This repository is intentionally public because Apple requires privacy policy and support URLs to be reachable by reviewers and users. It must not contain private operational materials, private business logic, credentials, or unreleased operational documents.

LensLog pages must keep clear estimate boundaries: calorie values are not medical or nutrition treatment advice, and expense values are not accounting, tax, banking, investment, or financial advice.
